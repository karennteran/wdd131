Personal Budget planner

This name was chosen due to that it specifies exactly what the website is about.

Site Purpose

The Personal Budget Planner is a website designed to help individuals manage their finances effectively. It serves as a tool to track income, expenses, and savings goals while providing a user-friendly interface to visualize spending habits. By organizing financial data and offering insights, the site empowers users to make informed decisions about their money.The website will have three main pages, a HOME page a BUDGER TRACKER page, and a FINANCIAL TIPS & TOOLS page.On the home page, users are welcomed with an introduction to the site and its purpose. The navigation is clear and intuitive, allowing visitors to easily move between sections. This pafe also highlights key features, such as dynamic expense tracking and savings goal calculator, to encourage users to explore further.The budget tracker page is the core functionality of the project. Users can input their income and expenses into a form, specifying details like category, description, and amount. As they add entries, the site will dynamically update a table displaying all their financial data. Calculations for total income, total expenses, and the remaining balance will happen in real-time. The tracker will also store this data in the browser using local storage, so users can return to the site later and pick up where they left off. To make budgeting more visual, the page will feature a chart (like a pie or bar graph) that breaks down expenses by category, helping users see their money.The financial tips & tools page will provide users with advice for better management of money. There will be tips for saving, reducing expenses, and creating a sustainable budget. A saving goal calculator will allow users to input a financial goal, their current savings, and monthly contributions to calculate how long it will take to reach their target. The site will also offer motivational feedback based on the users input, such as suggestions to adjust contributions to hit their goal faster. The Personal Budget Planner is more than just a website, its a practical tool for managing money with ease and confidence, its dynamic, user-friendly features make budgeting less intimidating and more visual to have better control of finances. By helping users track their finances and achieve their goals, the project aims to improve financial literacy while serving as a foundation for potential growth into a broader business venture.
